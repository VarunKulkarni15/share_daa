Direct Beam

Instantly transfer files over your local Wi-Fi. No internet required, no cloud limits, no wait times. Just pure, unthrottled peer-to-peer bandwidth.

How it works: The desktop app generates a local QR code. Scan it with any mobile device or tablet to instantly link them together. Drop a file, and it beams straight across!

Zero-Cloud Privacy.

ShareDaa routes your files through a high-speed Rust backend directly on your local Wi-Fi. There are no middleman servers, no cloud telemetry, and no sign-ups. Your data stays entirely in your hands.

System Requirements

Built with Tauri (the "Anti-Electron"), ShareDaa is incredibly lightweight and uses a fraction of the RAM of traditional apps. It runs perfectly on nearly any machine.

Minimum Specs

  • OS: Windows 10 or 11
  • CPU: 1.5 GHz Dual-Core
  • RAM: 1 GB
  • Storage: 50 MB Free Space

Recommended Specs (For huge files)

  • Network: Gigabit Wi-Fi 6 Router
  • CPU: 2.0 GHz Quad-Core+
  • RAM: 4 GB+
  • Storage: Solid State Drive (SSD)
