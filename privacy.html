Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

At ShareDaa, we believe that true privacy is built into the architecture, not just written in a legal document.

Because ShareDaa is a True Peer-to-Peer (P2P) application designed exclusively for your local network, our privacy policy is incredibly simple: We collect absolutely nothing.

1. Zero Cloud Architecture

ShareDaa does not use third-party cloud servers to route, store, or relay your files.

  • Direct Transfers: When you send a file, the data travels directly from your device to the recipient's device using raw, local WebSockets over your internal Wi-Fi network.
  • No Middlemen: Your files never leave your local LAN. There are no STUN/TURN relays, no cloud storage, and no proxy servers.

2. Zero Data Collection

Because we do not operate a centralized backend, we do not have the technical capability to collect:

  • Your IP address or location data.
  • The names, sizes, or contents of the files you transfer.
  • Usage analytics, metadata, or crash reports.
  • Account information (ShareDaa requires absolutely zero sign-ups or logins).

3. Secure Local Routing

ShareDaa generates a dynamic, one-time 4-digit PIN and QR code to pair devices. These codes contain your local IPv4 address and allow devices to establish a direct, closed-circuit connection within your home or office network.

4. Future Monetization (Ads)

While ShareDaa currently operates with zero data collection, we reserve the right to introduce non-intrusive advertisements in future updates. If integrated, we will only use ad networks that comply with Microsoft Store policies, and we will update this privacy policy to reflect any anonymous usage data required by those ad networks.

5. Open Source & Verifiable

ShareDaa's source code is completely public. We encourage security researchers and users to audit our codebase to independently verify that no telemetry, trackers, or hidden network calls exist.
